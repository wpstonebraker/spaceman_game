    After a routine scientific expedition to mars, Dad begins to make his way home... when he encounters a massive alien base! As the nearest Space Force outpost, you have control over his ship's defense systems. You alone can save Dad. Will Space Dad be home in time for dinner? Or will Space Dad be... dinner?
    Space Dad is turn-based card game. Aside from the enemy ship (provided by https://ko-fi.com/norma2d) and the explosion effect (JROB774 Joshua "JROB774" Robertson of opengameart.org), all assets and animations were drawn (badly) by me. Your premade deck includes 6 attack cards, 3 defensive cards, and your choice of a power card. You begin the game with 3 energy per turn; each card has an energy cost that detracts from that turns energy. Once you're out of energy (or before, if you'd like), click the "End Turn" button and the enemy will play their turn. At the start of your turn, you will draw 5 new cards from your deck of 10 cards.
