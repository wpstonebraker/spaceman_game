    After a routine scientific expedition to mars, Dad begins to make his way home... when he encounters a massive alien base! As the nearest Space Force outpost, you have control over his ship's defense systems. You alone can save Dad. Will Space Dad be home in time for dinner? Or will Space Dad be... dinner?
    Space Dad is turn-based card game. Your premade deck includes 9 "basic" cards: 3 laser attack cards, 3 missle attack cards, and 3 shield recharge cards. For each power card that you select beyond the first, 1 each of the basic cards will be added to your deck as filler. You begin the game with 3 energy per turn; each card has an energy cost that detracts from that turn's energy. Click the "End Turn" button and the enemy will play their turn. At the start of your turn, you will draw 5 new cards from your deck.

For each power card (below) that you select beyond the first, 1 each of the basic cards will be added to your deck as filler. Once you're out of energy (or before, if you'd like), click the "End Turn" button and the enemy will play their turn. At the start of your turn, you will draw 5 new cards from your deck of 10 cards.
